About us

Who hasn’t lost something in their life? All of us, at some time or another, have had that feeling of panic when we realize we’ve misplaced something. We retrace our steps, look in every nook and cranny and sometimes decide it must have sprouted legs and walked away.

This is the place to post Lost and Found Items in Kenya. We seek to make it easy for anybody to trace their lost items.

Often times, we find the lost item right where it was supposed to be. Other times, however, it ends up in the land of lost. While we can’t answer that question for you, we can let you know which items to keep an extra eye on. That is, after doing some research we’ve discovered the five most common items in are Lost/Misplaced

Items can be one of the following:-

  • Keys
  • USB Flash Drives
  • Mobile Phones/Tablets/Laptops
  • Certificates
  • Identification Documents/Receipts

So what can we learn from this? Well first, it’s that we need to keep better tabs on these five items. And second, we know that we’re not alone. Apparently everyone has problems keeping track of their keys, cell phone, Wallet, etc. Have you ever lost any of these items? Or have you lost something more valuable? Tell us your stories!
